Welcome to Zac's Academy Awards Guessing Game

This game is part of a research project I'm doing, discussing whether or not the Academy Awards accurately represent the tastes of contemporary audiences.

You will be given two Best Picture nominees from the last decade. Your job is to choose which one has the higher aggregate audience score (based on online review platforms)

You also have the option to play based on critic scores, rather than audience scores. This can be toggled in the top right.

Which of these Best Picture Nominees is preferred by General Audiences?

The general audience score for each film is an aggregate from iMDB, RT Audience Score and MC Audience Score
